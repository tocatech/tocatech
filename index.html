FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need a smartphone app?

No. Alerts are delivered directly to your email address. Setup is done through a web page that opens automatically on any device.

What happens if my WiFi goes down?

The device will attempt to reconnect automatically. Alerts will resume as soon as the connection is restored. You will not receive alerts while offline.

Can I control when I receive alerts?

Yes. Once activated you will receive a link in every alert email to manage your settings. You can set active hours (e.g. only alert between 22:00 and 06:00) and a minimum cooldown between emails.

How does the device get updates?

Firmware updates are pushed automatically over WiFi (OTA). Your device checks for updates every 6 hours and installs them silently without any action required from you.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contract. Plans are month-to-month and can be cancelled at any time. The device remains yours.

What is the range of the PIR sensor?

The sensor has a detection range of approximately 7 metres and a 120° field of view. Optimal placement is in a corner at head height.
