📡 Nightly WiFi Device Report
Between 2am and 6am, the device counts phones on your WiFi and sends you a nightly report. If tonight's count is higher than last night's, it sends an alert instead.
Default is 2. Set to 0 to get a report every night regardless of count.
🚬 Cigarette Smoke Detection
The device uses a PM2.5 air quality sensor to detect cigarette smoke. When sustained elevated particulate levels are detected, you receive an immediate alert email with air quality readings.
⚠ It is strongly recommended to keep the smoke sensor enabled at all times.
